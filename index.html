Resume

Hello my name is José Pedro Faria!

I'm Portuguese. I was born in 1999. And I believe that creativity and adaptability are the two most important skills for our future.

At the age of 15 I left my parents' house to study electronics, automation and computers, 200km away from home. At the age of 18, I entered a higher technical course in the integration of telecommunications systems and services. Later, at the age of 20, I entered Computer Science and Telecommunications, where I am currently.

I'm not at all a fan of the education system, at least in Portugal, I never tested another one, because I feel that it kills creativity and is not very flexible in absorbing new skills. It works the same way 50 years ago, but the world is not the same as it was 50 years ago. And just as I believe that adaptability is an essential skill for the future, I believe that the education system should adopt it.

I don't know how or when, but I will contribute to improving our society...
